POLICY DOCUMENT: STAR HEALTH COMPREHENSIVE PLAN
Insurer: Star Health and Allied Insurance Co. Ltd.

1. INTRODUCTION
This Policy is a comprehensive health insurance contract between the Insured and Star Health Insurance. It provides coverage for medical expenses incurred during hospitalization for illness or injury.

2. PREMIUM STRUCTURE
The annual premium for this plan is determined based on the age of the primary insured:
- For Age 30: ₹12,000 per annum (exclusive of GST)
- For Age 45: ₹18,000 per annum (exclusive of GST)
Premiums are subject to revision based on regulatory guidelines and age-slab transitions of the insured persons.

3. SUM INSURED (COVER AMOUNT)
The maximum limit of indemnity for all claims during the policy period is ₹5,00,000 (Five Lakhs Only). This is the base sum insured available to the policyholder.

4. SCOPE OF COVERAGE (INCLUSIONS)
This policy covers the following medical expenses, subject to the terms and conditions:
- In-patient Hospitalization: Full coverage for room, boarding, and nursing expenses.
- Day Care Procedures: Coverage for all medical treatments involving less than 24 hours of hospitalization due to technological advancements.
- OPD Cover: Reimbursement for Outpatient consultation fees, diagnostic tests, and pharmacy bills up to specified limits.
- Maternity Cover: Expenses related to childbirth are covered after a continuous waiting period of 2 years (24 months) under the policy.
- Pre & Post Hospitalization: Medical expenses incurred 60 days before and 90 days after hospitalization.

5. WAITING PERIODS
- Initial Waiting Period: 30 days from the policy inception date (except for accidental injuries).
- Pre-existing Conditions (PED): A mandatory waiting period of 2 years (24 months) applies to all pre-existing medical conditions disclosed at the time of proposal.

6. EXCLUSIONS (WHAT IS NOT COVERED)
- Cosmetic or Plastic Surgery: Unless required as part of reconstructive surgery following an accident or burns.
- Self-inflicted Injuries: Treatment for injuries resulting from attempted suicide or intentional self-harm.
- War Injuries: Illness or injury caused directly or indirectly by war, invasion, or warlike operations.

7. SUB-LIMITS AND CO-PAY
- Room Rent Cap: Room and boarding expenses are capped at 1% of the sum insured per day (₹5,000 per day for this plan).
- Co-payment: A mandatory 10% co-payment applies to all claims originating from Tier-3 cities or smaller towns.

8. CLAIMS AND NETWORK
- Claim Type: Cashless facility is available across our extensive network of 11,000+ hospitals in India. For non-network hospitals, the claim will be settled via reimbursement.
