OFFICIAL POLICY SUMMARY: NIVA BUPA REASSURE 2.0
Insurer: Niva Bupa Health Insurance Company Limited

PRODUCT OVERVIEW
ReAssure 2.0 is a premium health insurance product designed for broad-spectrum medical security, featuring unique lock-in premiums and low waiting periods.

KEY BENEFITS & FINANCIALS
- Annual Premium: ₹15,000 (Base rate for adult 18-35 age group)
- Total Cover Amount: ₹10,00,000 (Ten Lakhs)
- No-Claim Bonus: 50% increase in sum insured for every claim-free year, up to 100%.

SPECIFIC COVERAGE (OPD & MODERN TREATMENTS)
- OPD Case: Full reimbursement for outpatient consultation, diagnostics, and doctor-prescribed tests up to ₹5,000 per year.
- Modern Treatments: Full coverage for robotic surgeries, stem cell therapy, and oral chemotherapy.
- Air Ambulance: Covered up to ₹2,50,000 per hospitalization.

WAITING PERIODS (INDUSTRY LEADING)
- Pre-existing Diseases (PED): Coverage starts after just 1 year (12 months) of continuous policy coverage. This is significantly lower than the industry standard of 3-4 years.
- Specific Illnesses: 1-year waiting period for cataracts, hernia, and joint replacements.
- Initial Waiting: 30 days.

GEOGRAPHICAL TERMS & CO-PAY
- Co-Payment: 0% (Zero) co-pay for all treatments conducted in Metro cities and Tier-1 locations.
- Zonal Pricing: Premiums are calculated based on the city of residence. Treatments in non-declared zones may attract a 10% co-pay unless the treatment is for emergency accidental injury.

EXCLUSIONS
- General Exclusions: Routine eye exams, cosmetic enhancements, gender reassignment surgery, and experimental treatments.
- Intentional Harm: No coverage for suicide attempts or criminal activities.

CLAIM SETTLEMENT
- Live Track: Real-time claim status tracking via the Niva Bupa mobile app.
- Direct Settlement: Direct payment to 10,000+ network hospitals. 
- Fast Track: Guaranteed claim decision within 2 hours for cashless requests.
